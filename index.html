How to play

In the wacky world of Rock, Paper, Scissors, Fire, Water, you battle the all-knowing computer by choosing one of five options: Rock crushes Scissors and Fire, Paper smothers Rock and Water, Scissors snip Paper and Water, Fire burns Paper and Scissors, and Water douses Fire and erodes Rock. If both pick the same, it's a tie! Outsmart the computer and emerge victorious with the perfect pick. Good luck!
